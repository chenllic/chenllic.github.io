About

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Kageyama Lab, Department of Chemical Science and Engineering, Kyoto University.

Prior to this, I earned my PhD degree in Materials Science and Engineering at Center for High Pressure Science and Technology Advanced Research (HPSTAR).

My current research focuses on low-dimensional van der Waals magnetic materials. I employ mixed-anion strategies to design and synthesize novel low-dimensional magnetic systems. Beyond that, I utilize external stimuli—particularly pressure—to systematically modulate their physical properties, aiming to uncover emergent quantum phenomena, including superconductivity, spin crossover, and charge transfer.
